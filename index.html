WHO IS THIS GUY?

Jan-David Soutar is a Los Angeles-based filmmaker, writer, and occasional performer who creates work that's equal parts hilarious, disturbing, and strangely beautiful. With credits ranging from music videos to feature films to experimental theater, he refuses to be pigeonholed into any one category.

His work has been described as "what would happen if David Lynch and John Waters had a baby who was raised by Tim & Eric" (this is a real quote from someone probably). Whether he's directing surreal music videos, writing absurdist plays, or crafting unsettling short films, Jan-David brings a unique voice that's impossible to ignore.

THEATER & PERFORMANCE

Because sometimes actors need to suffer live in front of an audience. Jan-David's theatrical work blends absurdity with deep emotional resonance (or so he claims).
